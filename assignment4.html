Assignment 4: Job Fair!

"I've attended the job fair!"

The job fair was quite an experience! It was bustling with energy, with a wide range of companies from different industries. I had the opportunity to speak directly with several recruiters, which was incredibly valuable. I learned a lot about potential job openings and what these companies are looking for in candidates. There were also some useful workshops on resume writing and interview skills that I attended. It was a bit overwhelming at times, given the sheer number of attendees and the need to stand out, but overall, it was a productive day. I made some great connections and have a clearer idea of the next steps in my job search. Definitely worth attending if you're looking to explore job opportunities or expand your professional network.
